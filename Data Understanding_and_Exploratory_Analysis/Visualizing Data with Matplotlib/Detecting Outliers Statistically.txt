Detecting Outliers Statistically

combine multiple approaches for robust detection.

1. Visual Inspection
- Box plots, scatter plots and histogram reveal distribution patterns.

2. Z-Score Method
- Identiies values beyond three standard deviations from the mean.

3. IQR Method
- Flags values outside 1.5x interquartile range from quartiles.

Example: IQR-based detection

Q1 = df["salary"].quantile(0.25)
Q3 = df["salary"].quantile(0.75)
IQR = Q3 - Q1
outliers = df[
	(df["salary"] < (Q1 - 1.5IQR) |
	(df["salary"] < (Q3 + 1.5IQR)
]
