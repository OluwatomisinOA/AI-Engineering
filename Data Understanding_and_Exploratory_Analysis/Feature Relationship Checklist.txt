Your Practical EDA Workflow

1. Compute Correlation Matrix
- start with df.corr() to get the full picture of feature relationships across your dataset,

2. Visualize Heatmaps
- transform the matrix into visual form. Loook for red/blue clusters indicating strong positive/negative correlations

3. Identify Top Target Features
- sort correlations with your target variable. These features are your prediction power players.

4. Check for multicollinearity
- look for high inter-feature correlations (|r| > 0.8). Decide whether to address based on your modeling approach.

- Create Scatter Plots 
- don't rely on correlation alone. Visualize key relationships to catch non-linear patterns and outliers.

- Develop Feature Strategy 
- based on your analysis, decide: Which features to keep? Which features to transform? Document your reasoning.
