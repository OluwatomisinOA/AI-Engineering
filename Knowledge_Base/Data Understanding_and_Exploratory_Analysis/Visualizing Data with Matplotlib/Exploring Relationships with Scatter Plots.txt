Scatter plots reveal relationships between two continuous variables by plotting individual data points in two-dimensional space. Each point's position encode values for both variables, making patterns of association immediately visible.

1. Identify Correlation
- Spot negative, positive, or no correlation between features instantly through the overall point pattern.

2. Discover Clusters
- Reveal natural groupings in your data that might suggest distinct segments or categories.

3. Detect Non-Linear Patterns
- Identify curved or complex relationships that simple correlation metrics might miss

Code Example:

plt.scatter(df["experience"], df["salary'])
plt.xlabel("Experience")
plt.ylabel("Salary")
plt.title("Experience vs Salary")
plt.show()
