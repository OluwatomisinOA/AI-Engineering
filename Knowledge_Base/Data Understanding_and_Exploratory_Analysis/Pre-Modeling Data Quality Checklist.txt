Pre-Modeling Data Quality Checklist

before training any model, systematically verify your data quality. This checklist ensures you've addressed the most common data issues that can compromise model performance.

1. Assess Missing Data
- Check missing value counts and percentages per column. Visualize patterns across features.

2. Understand Missingness
- Investigate why values are missing. Determine if pattern is MCAR, MAR, or MNAR.

3. Choose Handling Strategy
- Decide on deletion, imputation, or advanced methods column by column based on context.

4. Detect Extreme Values
- Use statistical methods and visualizaions to identify potential outliers in numeric features.

5. Handle With Context
- Remove, cap, transform, or keep outliers based on domain knowledge and modeling goals.

6. Validate Results
- Re-check dataset statistics, distributions, and completeness after all cleaning operations.
